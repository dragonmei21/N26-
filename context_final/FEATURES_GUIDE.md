# Practical Features Guide

This guide breaks down the core features of the N26 AI Financial News Curator in plain English.

## 1. The Personalized Feed
Instead of a generic wall of news, the system analyzes the user's recent bank transactions to build a "Spend Profile" (e.g., "You spend mainly on tech subscriptions and urban transport"). 

When news articles are pulled in, an AI scores each article based on how relevant it is to the user's specific lifestyle, pushing the most personally impactful news to the very top.

## 2. The "Trace Impact" (Macro Reasoning Engine)
This is the flagship educational feature. When a major macroeconomic event occurs (like an "ECB Rate Decision" or "AI Chip Crisis"), the user can click **Trace Impact**.

Instead of a long essay, the AI generates a simple, 4-step "Causal Chain". It visually maps out exactly how an obscure central bank decision ripples through the economy, affects specific industries, and ultimately influences the stock price of companies the user cares about. It fetches real historical performance data for the involved stocks to prove its point.

## 3. "Explain Like I'm 10" (ELI10)
Financial jargon can be intimidating. At any point, a user can tap an "ELI10" button on complex topics to get a friendly, simple analogy explaining the concept without any confusing terminology.

## 4. Audio Podcasts ("On-the-Go" Summaries)
For users who prefer listening over reading (e.g., during a commute), the app automatically generates a cohesive daily "podcast" script highlighting the top stories and their personal impacts. It then uses AI Text-to-Speech (TTS) to generate a high-quality audio summary that the user can play directly in the app.

## 5. Pool Portfolios Integration
The app doesn't leave the user hanging after reading the news. If a user traces the impact of a tech boom and learns that semiconductor stocks are rising, the UI seamlessly presents related "Pool Portfolios" (e.g., a "Tech Infrastructure" basket). This creates a direct pipeline from *learning* to *investing*.

## 6. Smart Notifications
A notification bell alerts users to breaking macro events and freshly generated personalized insights, drawing them back into the app at the most critical moments in the market.
