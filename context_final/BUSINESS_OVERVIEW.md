# Business Overview: N26 AI Financial News Curator

## The Problem
Modern retail investors and banking clients are constantly bombarded with macroeconomic headlines—interest rate hikes, tech earnings, geopolitical tensions, and supply chain disruptions. While they know these events are important, they often fail to understand **how they impact their personal wallets** or **how they should act** on this information. The bridge between "global news" and "personal finance" is broken.

## The Solution
The **N26 AI Financial News Curator** is a personalized, AI-driven financial feed designed to live inside a modern banking app ecosystem. It does not just aggregate news; it **translates** news into actionable, personal insights.

By leveraging a user's transaction history (their "spend profile") and state-of-the-art Large Language Models (LLMs), the application curates the most relevant financial news and explains *exactly why it matters to them*.

## Core Value Proposition
1. **Financial Empowerment**: Demystifies complex economic jargon (e.g., "Yield Curve Inversions" become "Why your mortgage might get cheaper").
2. **Deep Personalization**: A user who spends heavily on subscriptions and gaming receives news framed around the tech sector, whereas a frequent traveler sees the personal impact of oil prices.
3. **High Engagement**: By offering bite-sized insights, casual chains ("Trace Impact"), and on-the-go audio podcasts, the app transforms passive reading into regular, daily app engagement.
4. **Action-Oriented Pipeline**: Seamlessly connects educational news to investment actions (e.g., "If this news makes you bullish on tech, here is a Tech Giants portfolio").

## Target Audience
- **The "Curious Novice"**: Wants to learn about investing but gets intimidated by traditional financial jargon.
- **The "Busy Professional"**: Has some money to invest but lacks the time to decipher what the Federal Reserve's latest meeting means for their portfolio.
- **The "On-the-Go User"**: Prefers listening to audio summaries during their commute rather than reading long articles.

## Business Impact
By turning news into personalized insights, N26 can bridge the gap between their daily banking users and their investment products. This tool drastically reduces the friction of first-time investing by educating the user *in context* step-by-step.
